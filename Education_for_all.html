Education Projects (by Year)

Explore our year-by-year education initiatives — click a card to see detailed impact for that year.

Our education program aims to provide sustainable access to quality learning opportunities for underserved youth. We distribute scholarships to deserving students across primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions. The program also includes school feeding initiatives to ensure students are well-nourished and focused on learning, as well as the provision of learning materials such as textbooks, notebooks, and writing supplies. Additionally, we support teacher training and infrastructure improvements to create conducive learning environments that foster academic excellence and character development.

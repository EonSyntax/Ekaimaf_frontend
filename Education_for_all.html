EDUCATION FOR ALL

We support schools, fund scholarships, and promote literacy across underserved areas.

Focused on improving access to quality education, this program supports vulnerable children and young adults by providing essential learning materials through school support drives. It also operates scholarship schemes for academically gifted but financially constrained students. Furthermore, the program champions literacy improvement campaigns, such as reading clubs, adult literacy classes, and teacher support initiatives, all aimed at increasing literacy levels and fostering a culture of lifelong learning.

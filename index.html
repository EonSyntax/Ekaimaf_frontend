About ECAF

Driven by Compassion. Committed to Change.

Ekaima Charitable Aid Foundation (ECAF) is a non-profit organization transforming lives through sustainable development. We empower communities across Africa to rise above hunger, poverty, and inequality—building a future rooted in dignity, opportunity, and hope.

Zero Hunger Projects (by Year)

Explore our year-by-year hunger relief initiatives — click a card to see detailed impact for that year.

The aim of this initiative is to eliminate hunger and reduce malnutrition in vulnerable communities. The program runs feeding schemes for children, the elderly, and low-income families, ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry. To build sustainability, it supports the creation of community farms and gardens, enabling families to grow their own food while enhancing local food security. Complementing these efforts is nutrition education, which teaches communities how to prepare balanced meals, preserve food, and adopt healthier eating habits.
